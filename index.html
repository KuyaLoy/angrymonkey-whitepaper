Connecting Wallet and Signing Up

Note: While AngryMonky supports MetaMask and Coinbase Wallet, this manual only covers how to use the marketplace using MetaMask. Another version of the manual for Coinbase Wallet will be available later

Connecting MetaMask Wallet

You must have a MetaMask wallet installed on your device. You can download it from the official website. If you already have the wallet, follow the steps:

  1. Go to AngryMonky’s website: https://angrymonky.com/
  2. Click on Connect Wallet, then choose MetaMask.
  1. Wait until your Metamask appears, and choose which MetaMask account you want to connect.
  1. On MetaMask, click Next, then Connect.
  1. Choose your preferred blockchain to connect the wallet.*

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. On your MetaMask, sign the message that will appear to you by clicking Sign (No charges are applied).

Complete Signing Up

  1. Add a username.
  2. Add your email.*

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. Agree to AngryMonky’s Terms of Service by selecting the checkbox.
  2. Click on Complete Sign-up.

Claiming Migrated NFTs (STRMNFT Users Only)

Verify Your Email

  1. Open the dropdown menu in the top right corner near the notification icon and click on Profile.
  1. 2. Near your username, click on Verify Email.
  1. Make sure your entered email address is the same one you used on STRMNFT.
  2. Click on Send OTP
  1. Near your username, click on Verify Email.
