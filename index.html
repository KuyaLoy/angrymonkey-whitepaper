Connecting Wallet and Signing Up

Note: While AngryMonky supports MetaMask and Coinbase Wallet, this manual only covers how to use the marketplace using MetaMask. Another version of the manual for Coinbase Wallet will be available later

Connecting MetaMask Wallet

You must have a MetaMask wallet installed on your device. You can download it from the official website. If you already have the wallet, follow the steps:

  1. Go to AngryMonky’s website: https://angrymonky.com/
  2. Click on Connect Wallet, then choose MetaMask.
  1. Wait until your Metamask appears, and choose which MetaMask account you want to connect.
  1. On MetaMask, click Next, then Connect.
  1. Choose your preferred blockchain to connect the wallet.*

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. On your MetaMask, sign the message that will appear to you by clicking Sign (No charges are applied).

Complete Signing Up

  1. Add a username.
  2. Add your email.*

*Important: If you’re an STRMNFT user, you must enter your STRMNFT email address to be able to claim your migrated NFTs on AngryMonky.

  1. Agree to AngryMonky’s Terms of Service by selecting the checkbox.
  2. Click on Complete Sign-up.

Claiming Migrated NFTs (STRMNFT Users Only)

Verify Your Email

  1. Open the dropdown menu in the top right corner near the notification icon and click on Profile.
  1. Near your username, click on Verify Email.
  1. Make sure your entered email address is the same one you used on STRMNFT.
  2. Click on Send OTP
  1. Enter the OTP sent to your email and click on Verify.

Claim Your Migrated NFTs

  1. On your Profile section, click on Migrate.
  2. Choose a collection.
  1. You can either click on NFTs to select individually or click on Select All to select all available NFTs in the collection.
  2. After selecting, click on Migrate.
  1. A new pop-up will appear with details about the blockchain and the required fees. Click on Continue.
  1. Confirm the transaction by clicking on Confirm in your MetaMask.*

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. Sign the message in your MetaMask (if required).
  1. Your migration request has been submitted. Upon approval, you’ll be able to see your migrated NFTs on AngryMonky.*

*Note: You need to repeat the migration process for each collection separately.

Buying NFTs

Buy NFTs at a Fixed Price

  1. Find the NFT you wish to buy, then click on Buy Now.
  1. You will see a pop-up with the price details, click on Continue.
  1. Wait until processing the payment is complete.*

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. On your MetaMask, click on Confirm to approve the payment transaction.
  1. After approving the transaction, wait a few seconds for the purchase to be finalized.
  1. Once the pop-up says “Completed”, the NFT will then be owned by you.

Place Bids on NFTs

Note: To place bids, you’ll need to use the wrapped version for some tokens (WETH, WBNB, WMATIC). If you don’t have sufficient balance, you’ll be prompted to convert to a wrapped token in your wallet. You can choose the amount you need and convert your tokens easily.

  1. Find the NFT you want to bid on, then click on Place Bid.
  1. Add your Bid Amount and choose the currency (it should be more than the Highest Bid).
  2. Set the expiration date (optional).
  3. Click on Confirm Your Bid to continue

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. On your MetaMask, click on Approve (if required).
  1. Sign the message in your wallet (if required).

Selling NFTs

Sell at a Fixed Price

  1. Go to the NFT you want to sell, and click on Put on Sale.

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. Choose Fixed Price and click on Proceed with your sale.
  1. Choose the currency and enter your price. Below, you can see how much you will receive from the sale.
  2. Click on Confirm your price.
  1. Confirm the transaction on your MetaMask and sign the message (if required).
  2. Click on Confirm your price.

Open for Bids

  1. Go to the NFT you want to open for bids, and click on Put on Sale.

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. Choose Open for Bids and click on Proceed with your sale.
  1. Set your Ask Price and choose a currency. Below, you can see how much you will receive from the sale.
  2. Click on Continue.
  1. Confirm the transaction on your MetaMask and sign the message (if required).

Creating a Collection

Create a New Collection

  1. Go to the homepage and hover on Create, then click on Collection.
  1. Choose the blockchain on which your collection will be created, then click on Create Collection.
  1. Add a profile and cover photos.
  2. Add a Collection Name and Ticker, then select a category.
  1. Add a Description for your collection.
  2. Enter a Short URL, which will be publicly visible.
  3. Add Social Media links for your collection.
  4. Make sure all the information is correct, then click on Create.
  1. On your MetaMask, click on Confirm and sign the message in your wallet (if required).
  2. Click on View Collection to go to the collection page.

Creating NFTs

Create a Single NFT

  1. Go to the homepage and hover on Create, then click on NFT.
  1. Choose the blockchain on which your NFT will be created, then click on Single NFT.

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. Click on Choose file to upload an image for your NFT.
  2. Enter a name for your NFT.
  3. Select the Type of Sale.
  4. Choose whether you want to use Free minting or pay the fees instead of the first buyer.
  5. Set the price and choose a currency.
  6. Set a royalty fee between 0% and 50% (you can enter a value with up to two decimals).
  1. Select the category and collection to which your NFT will be added.
  2. Add a Description for your NFT.
  3. Add Traits and Subtraits (They must be added individually for each NFT. If you want to add them all at once, contact support@angrymonky.com)
  4. Make sure all the information is correct, then click on Create.
  1. Wait until the media is uploaded, then sign the message in your MetaMask to confirm the minting and listing requests (if required).
  2. Click on View NFT to go to the NFT page.

Create Bulk NFTs

  1. Go to the homepage and hover on Create, then click on NFT.
  1. Choose the blockchain on which your NFT will be created, then click on Bulk NFTs.

*If your MetaMask prompts you to switch the network, click on Switch network to continue. If it prompts you to add a network, click Approve.

  1. Click on Choose files to upload images (you can choose up to 100 at a time).
  2. Enter the name that will be used for all minted NFTs.
  3. Select the collection to which your NFT will be added.
  4. Add a Description for your NFTs.
  5. Below, you’ll see the Total Bulk Fees you need to pay for bulk minting.
  6. Make sure all the information is correct, then click on Create.
  1. On your MetaMask, confirm the transaction by clicking Confirm.
  1. Wait until the images are uploaded, then sign the message in your wallet (if required).
  1. Click on View NFTs to find the new NFTs in your Profile section.

Managing Profile

Edit Profile

  1. Open the dropdown menu in the top right corner near the notification icon and click on Profile.
  1. Click on Edit Profile, next to the settings icon.
  1. Add Profile and Cover photos.
  1. You can edit your Username and customize your URL and Bio.
  1. Add your available social media links and click on Save

NFT Promotion

This feature allows you to request a paid promotion for your NFTs and make them visible on the homepage. To request a promotion, follow these steps:

  1. Open the dropdown menu in the top right corner near the notification icon and click on Profile.
  1. In the Owned tab, enable the Select for promotion option.
  1. Select the NFTs you want to promote, then click on Promote.
  1. Select the promotion type and dates.
  2. Select the payment currency.
  1. Confirm the payment in your MetaMask and sign the message (if required).

Add NFTs to MetaMask

  1. Open the dropdown menu in the top right corner near the notification icon and click on Profile.
  1. In the Owned tab, enable the Add to MetaMask option.
  1. Before selecting the NFTs you wish to add to MetaMask, please note the following:
    1. a. The selected NFTs must be on the same blockchain.
    2. b. The selected NFTs must be minted on the blockchain.
    3. c. Your MetaMask must be connected to the blockchain of the selected NFTs.
  2. After selecting the NFTs, click on Push.
  1. On your MetaMask, click on Add NFTs.
